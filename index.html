About Us

Where passion meets excellence in music education

Welcome to Sargam Music Academy, where rhythm meets passion. Founded by Maestro Ramesh Sharma in 2010, our institute has been a beacon for music lovers of all ages.

Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps or a performer looking to refine your craft, we nurture your talent with personal attention, modern techniques, and soulful training. We offer professional courses in Indian classical, Western, vocal, and instrumental music.

Our vision is to spread the joy of music and make learning it an unforgettable, transformative experience that lasts a lifetime.
